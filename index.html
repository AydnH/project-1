Household Fiber Connection.

Fiber Cable

Optical fiber cable is and assembly close to electrical cables but containing optical fibers that carry light. Optical fiber elements are usually covered with plastic and kept in protective tubes. They are used for Long distance telecomunication and providing high speed data connection.

Optical network terminal

An optic Network Terminal (ONT) connects you your modem and converts the light signals form fiber and converts them to electrical signals which your modem can then read. Allowing for wireless or hardwired connection.

Modem

Modem or modulator - demodulator is a network hardware device that modulates carrier wave signals to encode digital information for transmission and demodulates signals to decode transmitted data.(turning digital data of computers into modulated electrical signal for transmission over telephone or fiber optic lines.)

Ethernet Cable

a cable used for connecting a number of computers to a Local area Network(LAN) or Wide Area Network(WAN). Systems connected by ethernet divide streams of data into pieces called "frames" a frame contains source and destination addresses and error checking data. Creating a stronger connection with less latency.
